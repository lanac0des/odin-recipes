Quick Wrap

Description

Wraps are a versatile, nutritious, quick, and delicious meal choice. The ingredients can be modified to make them suitable for breakfast, lunch, dinner, and a snack! Typically, a wrap will consist of vegetables and proteins being wrapped inside of a wheat or flour-based flat circle. Wraps taste best when lined with a dressing or sauce that complements the ingredients.

The wrap you'll be learning to make today will include a tomato & grain wrap, a hearty helping of spring mix, cheddar cheese, ham, bell peppers, imitation crab, and the salad dressing of your preference. Ranch is recommended. With this assortment of ingredients, you'll be enjoying the benefits of nutrients such as calcium, protein, lycopene, vitamin K, and many others. This wrap takes about 5 minutes to make, it sustains one's apetite, and it's absolutely delicious!

Ingredients

  • tomato wrap
  • preferred salad dressing
  • spring mix
  • sharp cheddar cheese
  • honey ham
  • red bell pepper slices
  • imitation crab strips

Steps

  1. Wash and slice the bell pepper.
  2. Put the tomato wrap on a clean plate. Spread the salad dressing of your choice onto the wrap.
  3. Add a thick layer of spring mix on top of the dressing.
  4. Add two slices of cheese, and then two slices of ham on top of the cheese.
  5. Place the bell pepper slices down next, followed by the imitation crab strips.
  6. Add more dressing on top as desired.
  7. Roll the wrap around the ingredients and enjoy!
